about us

At Rufia Agro, we are committed to providing our customers with the freshest, highest-quality dairy products possible. Our family-owned farm has been in operation for over 50 years, and we take pride in our long history of excellence in farming. Our cows are raised on our lush green pastures and are never given antibiotics or hormones. We believe that happy, healthy cows produce the best milk, and our cows are treated with the utmost care and respect. We are passionate about sustainability and are constantly looking for ways to reduce our carbon footprint and protect the environment. From using renewable energy sources to recycling waste, we are always striving to be responsible stewards of the land. We invite you to come visit our farm and see for yourself how we operate. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community is what sets us apart, and we look forward to sharing our farm with you.
